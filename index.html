Aim:

First player to get three counters to the opposite 'Home' side wins.


Instructions:

For each turn, players can move their counters 3 times. Moves can be split amongst any of their counters in play in whichever way they like: one counter 3 times, or 3 different counters each once.

Counters can move forward, or sideways if an adjacent square has a symbol matching their current square. Counters cannot move backwards.

If a player encounters a counter of the opposite team, they are blocked (no one can move forward).

Within the 'Free' lane, a player can move their counter to any unoccupied square.

Players can only land on the 'Home' square if it is unoccupied by the other player.
